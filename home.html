Welcome to Srilanka!


Ayubowan-May you live long! That's our cherished wish to each and every one of you visiting our beautiful country. welcome to the charming island Srilanka where the warmth of the tropical sun and bright smiles of the warm hearted people is sure to envelope your body, mind and soul.Srilanka situated in the indian ocean is a tourist's delight with its endless wonders from beautiful beaches,mountain retreats,wildlife parks, ancient cultural monuments,nature reserves,magnificent scenic locations and incredible festivals.Srilanka is a land of endless wonders waiting to be experienced!





TryVilla

TryVilla is beautiful luxury beach villa situated by the sea in the quaint fishing village of koggala.This four bed roomed villa boasts of a beautiful view of the indian ocean,which is just a stone's throw away from the hotel.Take long walks on the soft sandy beach;sunbathe for the right ta or relax under a sunshade under a sunshade watching the many moods of the ocean.Don't miss the mesmerizing sunset as it paints the sky with rich hues of red and orange.If you love the ocean,then you are sure to love tryvilla.
